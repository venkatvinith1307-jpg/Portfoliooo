Trip to Varkala

Varkala is a famous coastal town in Kerala known for its cliffs adjacent to the Arabian Sea. I visited Varkala during my semester break with my friends.

The Varkala Cliff offers a breathtaking view of the sea. Watching the sunset from the cliff was the most beautiful part of the trip. The beach was clean and peaceful.
